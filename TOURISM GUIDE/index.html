About TravelTrack

TravelTrack is a unique tourism guide application that helps travelers discover amazing destinations while keeping track of their journey. Our mission is to make travel planning easier and more enjoyable by providing a comprehensive platform for exploration and documentation.

Our Features

  • Destination Discovery: Explore thousands of destinations worldwide with detailed information.
  • Journey Tracking: Keep a record of places you've visited and create a visual timeline of your adventures.
  • Wishlist Management: Save places you want to visit in the future.
  • Travel Statistics: View insights about your travel history and preferences.
  • Data Visualization: Interactive charts and graphs to visualize your travel patterns.
  • User Profiles: Create a personalized profile to store your travel memories.

How Traceability Works

Our unique traceability system allows you to:

  1. Mark destinations as visited or add them to your wishlist
  2. Record the dates of your visits
  3. Add personal notes and photos to each destination
  4. Generate a chronological timeline of your travels
  5. Track statistics about your travel patterns and preferences
  6. Visualize your travel data with interactive charts and graphs
